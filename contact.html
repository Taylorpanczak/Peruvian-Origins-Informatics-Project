Expanding Project Area

If you are interested in adding your archaeological site to the project area, contact the websites curator. If you have other questions or inquires please contact Taylor Panczak at Panczt54@gmail.com

3D models and file sizes

In order to have your archaeological site listed on this website, you are required to have a textured 3D model already prepared (OBJ, PNG, and MTL files only) and atleast a small paragraph providing a description of the artifact. Total file sizes for the 3D models must be less than 15mb. Site co-ordinates must be latitude and longtitude
